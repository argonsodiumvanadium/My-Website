About this Project

This website is an ad-free alternative to other anime sites that hosts all your favourite anime for you to watch with its music, mangas and muuch more It provides descriptions and scores of all titles, both courtesy of MyAnimeList, and efficiently categorizes them for ease of access.

Its aim is to become a complete and independent ecosystem for people who love anime

Why do I think we need this

I as for one am an anime enthusiast, I love the convulated characters, the bizzare settings, the twisted plot, the amazing animation.
Anime truly shows you something normal shows cannot, the "feels" of animes like haikyuu and hunter x hunter can not be acheived with ease in normal shows, the rich,intense and deep emotional truths and themes of animes like Neon genesis evangelion are very hard to portray in real movies.

But I encountered a recurring issue whenever I saw a good anime, it was the pain of finding data, like the OST and openings of the anime, remembering 3-4 manga sites to continue the manga.

In the end it just became a huge mess, jumping from MyAnimeList for anime reccomendations to seeing the anime in a streaming website to jumping from one manga site to another to find the complete manga, and then I had to open Youtube for the openings and OST, and this cycle was repeated forever.

So to arrange all this scattered data and break this cycle, I decided to create animegami a complete and independent ecosystem for anime lovers
